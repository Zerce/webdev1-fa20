Vmin Vmax

vmin uses the ratio of the smallest side. That is, if the height of the browser window is less than its width, 1vmin will be equivalent to 1vh . If the width of the browser is less than it's height, 1vmin is equvialent to 1vw . vmax is the opposite: it uses the largest side.
